Human Freedom Index

The Human Freedom Index is a comprehensive measure of personal and economic freedom worldwide. There are 76 distinct indicators in areas such as rule of law, security, religion, expression, and freedom to trade internationally.

Freedom correlated with wealth

The top ranked countries in 2017 are predominately wealthy nations in North America, Europe and Oceania. On the other end of the scale, many of the least free countries are in developing nations in Africa, although the Middle East dominates the bottom of the list as well.
